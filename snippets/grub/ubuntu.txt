*** Files to modify

/boot/grub/grub.cfg is autogenerated on this distrobution, need to modify
another file to add menu entries

Modify /etc/grub.d/40_custom to add new boot entries

Run

update-grub (update-grub2 is a symlink to this)

afterwards to update the entries

*** Enter menu entry
Hold shift in BIOS or escape in UEFI on startup.
