*** Cell phones

# Screens
Made from glass, common glasses are:
Gorilla, Dragontrail, Aluminosilicate, High-Alkali, Tempered, Laminated, Sapphire, Plastic

# Sensors
Common sensors include:
Accelerator, Gyroscope, Proximity, Compass, Ambient light, GPS

# Battery
Type of materials used:
Lithium Ion

# I/O
Connector slots:
SD slots (Micro, nano, etc)
Pogo-pins
USB

# Cameras
Resolutions usually are:
8 - 13 MP

# Modems
GSM, CDMA, LTE, GPS, GPS-A, GLONASS
