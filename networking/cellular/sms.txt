*** Sending SMS over email
Various mobile companies has an email address that you can send to to get your message forwarded as a SMS

The email is usually structured as:
phone_number@mobile-provider-sms-service.com

Recently, this service has been cut by alot of companies because of spam, but some will still work, so just have to try and see
