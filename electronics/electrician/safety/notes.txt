hot stick
a hot stick, or safety stick, is an insulated pole with a tool at the end of it.
The type of tool can vary for a wide variety of jobs including voltage testing, operating switches, replacing fuses, and tightening fasteners,
while not exposing a work crew to a large risk of electric shock.
